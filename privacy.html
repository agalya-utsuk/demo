Privacy Policy – Read-Able Extension

Effective Date: 2025-09-25

Overview

The Read-Able Chrome extension respects your privacy. This extension does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information, browsing history, or highlighted text. All readability computations are performed entirely within your browser on your local device.

Data Collection

No personal data is collected. The extension operates only on the text that you select within the current web page and calculates a readability score locally. This text is never sent to any external server.

Data Sharing

Since no data is collected, no data is shared with third parties.

Permissions

The extension requests minimal permissions necessary to provide functionality, such as access to the currently active tab when you invoke the context menu action. These permissions are used solely to compute readability and are never used to track or profile you.
